How It Began

Power in Code began as a small club at Masterman High School in Philadelphia, where a passionate group of students gathered to learn coding. As their enthusiasm grew, they shared their knowledge, leading to new chapters at Father Judge, Roman Catholic, Academy at Palumbo, and many more. With each addition, Power in Code transformed from a small club into a citywide organization, with a team of over 15+ trained high school students bringing the love for coding to more than 10 different schools

Roles

We are accepting intern applications from all Philly high school students

Chapter Leader

Your role is to manage, and organize all club meetings and activities in your school. You’re responsible for maintaining, starting up, advertising, and finding people to help run the club. If you want to become a Chapter Leader, please reach out and we can walk you through the process

Outreach

Your role is to grow our organization by networking, advertising, and contacting schools, community centers, libraries, and potential collaborators to find new places to teach. Other tasks may include but is not limited to drafting emails or grant writings for our organization

Instructor

Your role is to teach and educate our students. You should consistently attend our after-school programs currently held at 300 E Lehigh Ave, Philadelphia, PA 19125, and other teaching opportunities, experience in HTML/CSS is highly recommended.

Marketing

Your role as a Marketing Intern for Power in Code is to promote our programs and events effectively. You will manage social media accounts, create engaging content, design flyers, and manage the funds of our organization. Marketing training will be provided
